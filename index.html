Discover the Story Behind Every Street Name

Street Story AR is a cultural and smart tourism project that helps users explore why streets were named this way and learn the story behind them.

Explore the Idea

Project Idea

Our project connects technology with culture by presenting the stories behind selected street names in a simple and interactive way. The user can browse street names through the website and later use the camera feature to view extra information through AR markers.

Prince Sultan Street

Palestine Street

A symbolic name reflecting cultural and historical significance.

Why Street Names Matter

  • Reflect cultural identity
  • Preserve historical figures
  • Help navigation
  • Connect people to heritage
