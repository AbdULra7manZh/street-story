About Street Story

Street Story AR is an innovative project that uses augmented reality to reveal the hidden stories behind street names. By simply scanning a street sign, users can discover the history, cultural meaning, and significance behind it.

Our Goal

To connect people with the history and culture of their cities in a simple and interactive way.

Technology

We use web technologies, camera access, and future marker integration to recognize street names.

Vision

To make every street a story and every city an open museum.

This project offers a smart cultural experience.
